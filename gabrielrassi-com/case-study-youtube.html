Content Strategy & Execution

My approach centered on developing a comprehensive content strategy that combined daily market commentary with strategic weekly programming. The goal was to create consistent value while building systematic authority in the finance education space.

Daily Content Machine

We implemented a daily content strategy covering the most relevant topics in finance, investments, economics, and geopolitics. This consistent presence kept us top-of-mind for our audience while allowing us to capitalize on trending financial events.

  • Daily Market Commentary: Real-time analysis of financial events and market movements
  • Economic Trend Analysis: Deep dives into macroeconomic factors affecting investments
  • Geopolitical Impact: How global events affect financial markets and investment decisions
  • Investment Education: Strategic content aligned with AUVP School curriculum
